💰 Day 02 – Tip Calculator

This interactive tool helps you split a restaurant bill fairly based on a custom tip percentage. Just enter your details below!

🧮 How It Works

  1. Enter the total bill amount.
  2. Choose a tip percentage.
  3. Tell us how many people are splitting the bill.
  4. See how much each person needs to pay!
